Ceramics 101

This course will go over the basics of creating ceramic items such as figurines and mugs. It is our most popular course. In the spring we offer the advanced version of this course. Items you make may be put up for sale at our annual booth at the farmers market. Instructor: Ashley Williams Dates: August 15-October 16 Time: 7-8:30PM Fee: 550.00USD

Drawing 101

This course will go over the basics and techniques of drawing using graphite and charcoal mediums. In the spring we offer drawing 102 which will tackle more detailed projects. One project of your choice may be selected to be framed and sold at our booth at the farmer's market. Instructor: Jared Tran Dates: August 11-October 12 Time: 7-8:30PM Fee: 350.00USD

Painting 101

This course will take you through the common techniques of painting with acrylic and watercolors. Learn to paint still life and landscapes in this introductory course. One project of your choice may be selected to be framed and sold at our booth at the farmer's market. Instructor: Susan Youngblood Dates: July 14-September 13 Time: 7-8:30PM Fee: 400.00USD

Painting 102

This course will continue from painting 101 with acrylic and oil mediums. Examples of subjects include the human face, a reproduction, and a subject of your choice. Instructor: Susan Youngblood Dates: September 15-November 13 Time: 7-8:30PM Fee: 400.00USD

Pyrography 101

This course introduces you to the exciting world of making art on wood through burning techniques. Examples of images we will practice with include landscapes and floral patterns. One project of your choice may be selected to be sold at our booth at the farmer's market. Instructor: Daniel Novak Dates: August 15-October 13 Time: 8-9:30PM Fee: 500.00USD

Jewelry Making 101

This course introduces you to working with various tools and metals to create jewelry pieces. Participants in this course must be at least 18 years old. One project of your choice may be selected to be sold at our booth at the farmer's market. Instructor: Kristen Novak Dates: July 8-September 9 Time: 6:30-8PM Fee: 700.00USD
